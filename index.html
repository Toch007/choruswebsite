What The Chorus is all about

The chorus is simply a group of Choral Music lovers who decided to come together to take Choral Music in Abuja and Nigeria to a whole new level.
A harmony in unison, talents from different parts of the country, of various backgrounds and works of life.

Here are some of the reasons why we stand out, or should I say, are "Outstanding"

Professionalism

Music is a beauty in itself and making beautiful music is a serious business, thats where our professionalism comes in.
We make our music as professonal as possible without eliminating its beauty and the message that lies within.

Music Dynamics and Quality

The Chorus knows how to blend several musical parts and to blend in a balanced manner with the integration of four essential elements for a good performance namely: Intonation, diction, rhythm and balance.

Flexibility

Our performance cuts accross all genres of choral music, both foreign and indigenous, and also accross all musical eras.

Read Music

To reproduce or create good music, we sing it in its raw form, which is sheet music.
We also try to teach those who are not very conversant with staff notation to get the best quality of music.
