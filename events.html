Events

You Can Make It Happen Continuously

The Chorus Concerts and activities are made possible as a result of donations, contributions and support from its audience, members, local businesses, music lovers, and the community.

If you are a music lover and want to be a part of this great project of creating the greatest choral community in Africa, this is an opportunity to be a part of the next level of choral music.

The Chorus, a non-profit organization, relies on the generous contributions of its supporters in order to continue enriching our community with great music.

Donations realized by the chorus goes into the production of our concerts. They take care of:

  • Securing concert venues
  • Orchestra musicians
  • Set-up
  • Recording
  • Professional soloists & guest artists
  • Supporting members

and the list goes on and on. Your support is like a torch that shows the way in our music journey of uncertainties.

If you wish to join our family of supporters, you can send your donations by cash, cheques or donate online

Perks of being a sponsor

We solicit for sponsorship because we need your help. We need your help to enable us give back to the community by making many of our concerts free to the public or at a reduced rate. Just like we mentioned previously, these contributions help us pay for our venues, help us organise our set-up and sometimes our props, covers for our recordings, helps with our printing, and the list keeps going on and on. Our sponsors in return will gain exposure to our diverse audience. Sponsors will be listed on our media handles, website and on our printed programs. Any amount given towards sponsorship will be highly appreciated.
